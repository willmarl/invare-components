An analog conductivity-based liquid presence sensor (AKA wetness sensor). While often marketed as a "level sensor," it technically functions as a variable resistor that measures the total surface area currently bridged by a conductive liquid.

- Operating Voltage : 3.3V - 5V

It's a **water detector (how wet)**, not position-based.
The sensor has **exposed traces/pads** on the board that measures the **total surface area** currently bridged by a conductive liquid.
**It detects:**

- ✅ How wet the sensor pads are
- ✅ Water droplets on it
- ✅ Rain/moisture
- ❌ Water level at a specific height (Should use ultrasonic sensor instead)

**What the values mean:**

- **0-100**: Dry (no water contact)
- **100-500**: Damp (light moisture)
- **500+**: Wet (water touching pads)
